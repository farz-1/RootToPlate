Who We Are


Our Vision

For Scotland to be a place where all young people are given the opportunity
to have a rewarding future in work and life - no matter where they start their journey.

Our Mission

To inspire and equip young people, irrespective of their background, to learn, develop and reach their full potential through enterprise and financial education. We set high standards whilst being creative in our approach. Whilst continuing to grow our offering, we will ensure that all our activities are fit to meet our Mission ’whilst adhering to its guiding principle to do this through ’learning by doing.


Our Digital Vision

YES will enable and deliver on a clear digital transformation programme that will enable a clarity of communication, and programme offering via a future-proofed framework of blended learning that enables an appropriate and continually refreshed ongoing programme of enterprise learning to ensure equity of access.

Our Values

Our Values: In everything that we do, we will work with the following values:
- Integrity
- Enthusiasm
- Creativity
- Collaboration
